About BachatVikas

Our Vision

BachatVikas aims to empower individuals and families by providing trusted financial services. Our mission is to bridge the gap between Tier 1 cities like Mumbai and underserved Tier 2/3 cities by offering simplified financial solutions.

Who We Are

We are a SEBI-certified Mutual Fund Distributor with a vision to help Indians achieve financial growth. Currently, we are in the process of adopting advanced financial software like InvestWell Mint to enhance our mutual fund distribution and client experience.

Our Services

  • Mutual Funds & Investments
  • Loans & Credit Solutions
  • Insurance Advisory
  • Financial Literacy & Education

Our Focus

While headquartered in Mumbai, we are expanding into Tier 2 and Tier 3 cities across India, ensuring equal access to professional financial services for all communities.
