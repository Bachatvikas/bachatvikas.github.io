Why Financial Literacy Matters More Than Income

Many people believe earning more money will solve financial problems. In reality, financial literacy plays a much bigger role.

What is Financial Literacy?

Financial literacy means understanding how money works — budgeting, saving, investing, and managing risk.

High Income Without Knowledge

People with high income but poor money habits often struggle with debt, stress, and financial instability.

Low Income With Literacy

A person with modest income but good financial discipline can build stability, confidence, and long-term security.

Why Literacy Wins

  • Better decision making
  • Lower financial stress
  • More control over the future
